Nallamala Forest:

Nestled in the heart of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana states in India, Nallamala Forest is a paradise for nature enthusiasts and adventurers alike. Spanning over 5,000 square kilometers, it offers a diverse landscape of dense jungles, cascading waterfalls, and majestic hills. Home to a rich variety of flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, and rare bird species, it promises thrilling wildlife encounters. Visitors can indulge in trekking, wildlife safaris, and bird watching, while also exploring ancient temples and tribal settlements nestled within its lush greenery. Nallamala Forest invites you to immerse yourself in its untouched beauty and experience the raw essence of nature.

Best time to visit: November-february

How To Reach nallamala forest:

By Air:

Mangalore International Airport is the closest airport located 219 Kms away

By Train:

The nearest railway stations to Nallamala Forest are Markapur Road Railway Station and Nandyal Railway Station

By Road:

You can reach Nallamala Forest by road from major cities like Hyderabad, Vijayawada, and Kurnool.

Accomodation:

Forest Guest houses are provided with payment and camping is provided for adventurous travellers

SUNDARBANG FOREST:

One of the largest active deltas in the world, The Sundarbans, which derived its label from the Sundari Mangrove trees, is spread across India and Bangladesh. It is unique for its coastal mangrove forest covering 40,000 sq. km. With over 260 species of birds and the majestic black striped Royal Bengal tiger, the forest has 102 islands with 54 among them inhabited.

Best time to visit: August-December

How To Reach sundharban:

By Air:

The nearest major airport to the Indian Sundarbans is Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU) in Kolkata, West Bengal

By Train:

The nearest railhead to the Sundarbans is Canning Railway Station in West Bengal

By Road:

You can reach the Sundarbans by road from Kolkata or nearby cities like Diamond Harbour. From these places, you can take local buses or hire taxis to reach the Sundarbans

Accomodation:

Forest Rest Houses,Eco-Resorts,hotels and lodges exist
